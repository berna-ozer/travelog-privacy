Frequently asked questions

How do I add a country I've visited?

  • Tap the Map tab → tap any country → "Mark as visited"
  • Or import from photos: Home → Import from Photos. Travelog reads location data from your photos on-device.

How do I cancel my subscription?

  • iPhone Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Travelog → Cancel
  • Your premium access continues until the end of the current billing period.

I bought Lifetime — does it sync across devices?

  • Yes. Sign in with the same Apple ID on any device and your Lifetime purchase restores automatically.
  • If it doesn't, go to Profile → Restore Purchases.

How does the photo import work? Is it private?

  • Travelog reads the GPS coordinates embedded in your photos directly on your device.
  • Your photos never leave your phone. We only extract the location, not the image.
  • See our Privacy Policy for full details.

My streak broke — can I get it back?

  • Premium members can use Streak Rescue within 24 hours of breaking a streak.
  • Open Travelog → tap the streak rescue banner on Home.

How do I delete my account?

  • Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your data from our servers.
  • If you also want to cancel a subscription, do that in iPhone Settings first (deleting the account doesn't auto-cancel App Store subscriptions).

I'm not receiving notifications.

  • iPhone Settings → Travelog → Notifications — make sure they're enabled.
  • Inside Travelog: Profile → Notification Settings — check that the type you want is toggled on.

I found a bug or have a feature idea.

  • Email us at the address above — we love feedback and read everything.
